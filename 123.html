    About Eramed Engineering & Pharmaceuticals

    Company Overview

    Era Med Pharmaceuticals PLC is a leading pharmaceutical company based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Established in 2019, the company initially focused on importing and wholesaling medical equipment, medicines, and medical supplies. Over time, Era Med expanded into the manufacturing of medical instruments, appliances, and textile-based medical articles, solidifying its position as one of the fastest-growing healthcare companies in Ethiopia.

    Financial Growth

    Starting with an initial revenue of 5 million ETB in 2019, Era Med Pharmaceuticals has seen significant financial growth, reaching 134 million ETB in 2024.
